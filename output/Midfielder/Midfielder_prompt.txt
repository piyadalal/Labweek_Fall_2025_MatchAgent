A Midfielder is a player in a team sport played on a field. They are positioned in the central area of the field. Their main role is to act as a link, helping to move the ball from their team's own side towards the opponent's goal, and also to disrupt the opponent's efforts to score. They often cover a large portion of the field, moving between defense and attack.
