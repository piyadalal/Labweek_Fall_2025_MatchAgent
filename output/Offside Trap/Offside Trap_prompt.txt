An offside trap is a smart defensive strategy where a team's defenders intentionally move forward together at the same time. Their goal is to make an attacking opponent illegally 'offside' â€“ meaning the attacker is too far ahead of the ball and the last defender when a pass is made to them. By stepping up, the defenders leave the attacker behind their line, stopping the play.
